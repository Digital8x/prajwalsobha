Common Queries

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Sobha One World.

What is the exact location of Sobha One World?
Sobha One World is strategically located near Hoskote Toll Gate on Old Madras Road, East Bangalore, offering excellent connectivity to Whitefield, KR Puram, Budigere Road, and Kempegowda International Airport.
What are the apartment configurations available?
The project offers premium 1, 2, 3, and 4 BHK luxury residences catering to diverse lifestyle needs, spanning across 14 high-rise towers.
What is the total land area of the township?
Sobha One World is a massive integrated township spread across 48 sprawling acres, ensuring ample green spaces and world-class infrastructure.
What is the starting price of apartments here?
The starting price for a premium residence at Sobha One World is approximately ₹1.09 Cr onwards. Please refer to our pricing section or contact our experts for detailed pricing.
What lifestyle amenities does the project offer?
The project features world-class amenities including multiple swimming pools, tennis and pickleball courts, a rooftop cinema, a massive clubhouse, yoga decks, wellness zones, and dedicated kids' play areas.
Is the project RERA approved?
Yes, Sobha One World is a fully approved project. The RERA Registration number is PRM/KA/RERA/1250/304/PR/080526/008634.
What educational and healthcare facilities are nearby?
The location offers proximity to top educational institutions like VIBGYOR High School and Clarus International School, as well as renowned healthcare centers including Manipal Hospital ITPL and Aster Whitefield Hospital.
Is there adequate parking space available?
Yes, the project provides ample, secure, and dedicated covered parking spaces for residents, along with provisions for visitor parking.
What is the overall design theme of the project?
The township follows a contemporary, sustainable, and architectural luxury theme, emphasizing efficient layouts, natural light, cross ventilation, and lush green landscapes.
How can I book an apartment or schedule a site visit?
You can easily schedule a site visit or book an apartment by filling out the enquiry form on our website, clicking the "Enquire Now" button, or getting in touch with our experts via the Contact section.
